# Dashboard Notes

## Purpose

I created these charts to make the main results from the NYC 311 analysis easier to understand.

## Charts Included

- Complaints by borough
- Top 10 complaint types
- Average resolution time by borough
- Monthly complaint volume
- Monthly average resolution time
- Slowest common complaint types
- Agency workload

## Notes

- Brooklyn has the most complaints in the dataset.
- Staten Island has the fewest complaints.
- Illegal Parking is the most common complaint type.
- NYPD handles the most requests.
- Staten Island has the highest average resolution time.
- Monthly complaint volume stays fairly steady.
- Monthly resolution time changes more than complaint volume.
- New Tree Request has the longest average resolution time among common complaint types.
