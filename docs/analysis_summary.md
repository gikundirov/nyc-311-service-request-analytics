Analysis Summary
Overview

This document records observations from the project as the analysis progresses. New findings will be added as additional analyses are completed.

Dataset
2024 NYC 311 service request sample.
Records analyzed: 59,419
Complaint types: 148
Agencies: 14

Complaint Volume
Brooklyn had the highest number of complaints (19,051).
Staten Island had the fewest complaints (1,755).

Complaint Types
Illegal Parking was the most common complaint overall (9,578 requests).
Illegal Parking was the most common complaint in Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, and Staten Island.
HEAT/HOT WATER was the most common complaint in the Bronx.

Resolution Time
Staten Island had the longest average resolution time (21.10 days).
The Bronx had the shortest average resolution time (9.48 days).
New Tree Request had the longest average resolution time among common complaint types (330.26 days).

Monthly Trends
Monthly complaint volume remained consistent, with approximately 4,900–5,000 requests each month.
August had the longest average resolution time (17.17 days).
September had the shortest average resolution time (7.93 days).
